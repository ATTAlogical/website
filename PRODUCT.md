# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Three overlapping audiences, each entering through a different door:

- **atta logical visitors** — potential clients and business prospects, scanning for technical credibility. Professional intent: they want to know who built this and whether they can trust them.
- **laugical audience** — creative peers, collectors, and people drawn to material design. They're here to discover, buy, and be surprised. They engage slowly, not urgently.
- **ATTA.CKORE audience** — music listeners, industry contacts, collaborators. (Surface not yet designed.)

The site is also a portfolio of itself. Every surface is proof of concept for the design capability it represents.

## Product Purpose

A temporal, multi-brand creative ecosystem that proves technical competence through flawless execution and artistic integrity through distinctive interaction design. The site is simultaneously portfolio, store, music platform, and living art object.

Brand branches:
- **attalogical.com** — face of the business. Portfolio landing. First impression for professional contacts.
- **laugical** (/laugical or laugical.com) — creative and consumer branch. Store selling designed objects, one-of-one reworks, and curated items. The aesthetic IS the selection criteria.
- **ATTA.CKORE** — music catalog and releases. (Planned, TBD.)

Payment is handled via Stripe, custom-integrated. No Shopify, no templates.

## Brand Personality

Petallaugical — Fruitiger Surgical. Reflections, white surfaces, clinical precision, material honesty: objects and interfaces that reveal themselves rather than hide.

- **atta logical**: sterile, surgical, restrained. Competence proven by what's absent as much as what's present.
- **laugical**: experimental, expressive, sophisticated but not austere. Clinical precision applied to creative abundance. Frutiger Aero done with intention, not done mainstream.
- All branches share: Playfair Display, glass surfaces, temporal evolution engine, exploration-rewarded interaction.

## Anti-references

- Paper cutout and magazine letter-cutout aesthetics (compositional technique, not just the look)
- Generic e-commerce templates: Shopify default, WooCommerce default, anything that reads as a store kit
- Mainstream Frutiger Aero (trend-chasing version, not the aesthetic itself)
- Generic AI aesthetics: glassmorphism as decoration, hero-metric dashboards, identical card grids
- "Modern and clean" with no point of view

## Design Principles

**Functional beauty, decorative restraint.** Anything with a function — even just conveying information — must look good and work intuitively. Purely decorative elements are stripped to near-nothing. The contrast between richly designed functional surfaces and empty negative space is intentional.

**Time as material.** The temporal evolution engine drives a continuous, imperceptible drift in typography, position, and light across a 3-day cycle. Returning visitors see something slightly different. Time is not a feature — it is the medium.

**Discovery rewards curiosity.** Exploration and experimentation are repaid with surprise. Typing unexpected things, pressing things that don't look pressable, scrolling past what seems like the end — all of it should surface something. The model is Undertale: a world dense with hidden consequence, not just visible content.

**Petallaugical vision.** This is not a reference-driven aesthetic. The design language is singular: reflections, white surfaces, clinical precision, material honesty. Recognizable across all branches, irreducible to trend.
